Welcome to the game. For people who don't know about this game, let me tell you that this is game tests your memory retaining capability. So, to start the game, press any key from your keyboard. Then, any of the coloured buttons(red,blue,green,yellow)flashes. click on the flashed button, if you click on the correct button, you will land on to the next level. Then again another button flashes. But this time, you need to recall the button that you pressed in the previous level, and first click on that button and the current button that flashed. If you mess up, the game will get over and it will restart from level 0. Let's check how much can you score!!
